The gap

Most sites are not broken in one place. They leak demand across the whole journey.

Free tools usually show symptoms. SytFix connects those symptoms to crawlability, mobile behavior, local intent, trust signals, and revenue priority.

01

Google cannot fully interpret the business.

Thin service pages, weak schema, poor internal links, and duplicate location signals keep pages from earning qualified impressions.

02

Mobile visitors feel friction before they trust you.

Slow hero rendering, cramped forms, unstable layouts, and vague calls to action reduce leads before analytics can explain why.

03

AI search is changing discovery behavior.

Brands need structured, citable, consistent information that answer engines can verify, summarize, and associate with a service area.

What gets fixed

A roadmap built for implementation.
